About Me

Hi! My name is Huihuang Zheng. My name is extramely easy to remember because Huihuang means splendid or brilliant in Chinese. I’m a Chinese international student who comes from Xianyou County, Putian City, Fujian Province, China (福建省莆田市仙游县) So my first language is Puxian instead of Mandarin which is the Chinese official language. In Fujian, there was a very famouse man named Chenggong Zheng (郑成功). We have similar names: Chenggong means successful in Chinese. Our names are uncommon in China just like if you call someone Splendid Zheng or Successful Zheng in US ... So feel free to call me Huihuang Zheng, Splendid Zheng, or My English name Wayne Zheng.

I love Computer Science. That's my work, my interest, my love. My other interests including basketball and music. I'm currently a learner of ukulele.
